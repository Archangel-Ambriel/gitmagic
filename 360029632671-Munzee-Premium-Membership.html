Munzee Premium Membership

MunzeeSupport
  • July 01, 2020 15:12
  • Updated

While all players are able to enjoy Munzee free of cost, we are happy to offer many extra special features available upon purchasing a Premium Membership from the Freeze Tag Online Store. For the price of $30 a year, players will be able to enjoy the wide range of perks and benefits that come with Premium status.

FREE 7-day Premium Trial

All new players receive a FREE 7-day trial of the Premium Membership features upon creating an account. Note this only unlocks the FEATURES listed below. Upgrade to the full Premium Membership to unlock all the ITEMS below!

Premium Membership Advantages

Upgrading your existing Munzee account to a Premium Membership will offer the following benefits:

  • ITEMS
    • (1) Premium Munzee - This munzee is only available to Premium members and is worth 200 deploy points, 50 capture points, and 50 CapOn points. The Premium Munzee will be added to your credits list and be made available for immediate deploy once you have purchased a Premium Membership
    • (1) Blast Cap - Each Blast Capture may be used to capture up to 100 of certain virtual munzee types within a one mile radius of your current location. All Premium members will receive 1 courtesy Blast Cap, which you can read about HERE.
    • (2) Virtuals with (2) Virtual Color credits - Premium members will receive 2 Virtual Munzees, which can be deployed in places where it is impossible to hide a Physical Munzee. You can deploy these Virtuals via the app or online map. They will also receive 2 Virtual Color credits, which can be used to convert the color of the Virtual Munzees. 
    • (1) Flat Rob - The Flat Friends Munzees are based on members of staff. At the end of each month a scheduled is released determining when the Flat Friends will be on the map. All Premium members will receive 1 courtesy Flat Rob, which you can read about HERE. 
    • (1) Butterfly Evolution credit - Evolution Munzees are designed to provide new munzees to cap for players who don’t live in Munzee-dense areas. All Premium members will receive 1 courtesy Butterfly Evolution credit, which you can read about HERE.
    • 100 PinPoints per month awarded on the 15th of each month
  • FEATURES
    • Advanced Filters - One of the most celebrated aspects of the Premium Membership is the ability to use advanced filters to help with your capping adventures. For more information on filters, scroll to the bottom of the page!
    • Multi-User Login - Premium members are able to login up to 3 users at a time, providing a huge advantage for multi-player households and families.  
    • Expanded User Stats - Premium members will be able to go behind the scenes with detailed Munzee activity stats that they can view and share with other users. These are collectively known as Statzee.
    • Email Notifications - Premium members will be able to receive email notifications when a new munzee is deployed near them.
    • First Access - As an exclusive perk, Premium members will receive first access to some brand new products.
    • Leaderboard Access - Premium members are able to view the Daily Leaderboard complete with almost real-time player stats.
    • Titles - The ability to earn special titles on your profile for other players to view is available to Premium members.
    • Add Photos - Premium members have the ability to add pictures at the location of munzees for added fun. You can also earn exclusive badges for adding photos to munzees.
    • Added Ability - The ability to Process All/Delete All in Offline Queue is also available exclusively to Premium members.
    • Create Clan - The ability to create your own clan for Clan Wars 

Advanced Filter Advantages

While the many perks of a Premium Membership truly elevate the gaming experience, many players upgrade for the advanced filter options. There are many advantages to the advanced filters:

  • The ability to customize your own preset filters that you can activate instantly on the fly
  • Access to the special Premium Only filters, such as Myths and Specials, which can’t be easily located without advanced filters
  • The ability to hide the munzees you've captured by removing the red circles from the map, making it easier to find the ones you are yet to cap

Premium Membership Notes

Once a Premium Membership is purchased, players will receive a Premium Munzee in their credits list. The virtuals will appear in your undeployed list. The social is available under that tab and “My socials”. The expanded stats will become immediately available in your account. You might need to logout of your phone account and log back in to register the status update.

Enjoying the benefits of your Premium Membership? If you love the many perks and bonuses that accompany this upgrade, be sure to renew your membership after one year has passed. The great news is that you will receive all of the benefits and game pieces all over again upon annual renewal! We will remind you via email as that date approaches!
